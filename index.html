Beef Saikoro and Fried Rice

This meal is a delicious combination of Japanese style diced beed (Saikoro) paired with a flavorful cheesy fried rice which create a perfect comfort food combo that is surprisingly easy to make. It is budget friendly, filling, packed with flavor, as well as does not require much time to make, making it a perfect option for university students.

Chicken Wings

Fried chicken wings are a classic favorite crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside, and full of flavor. They are also easy to make at home with just a few ingredients, making them perfect for university students who want a satisfying meal or snack without spending too much.
